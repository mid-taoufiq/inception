Lies of P

The Tale of Krat

Lies of P is a thrilling action role-playing soulslike game that reimagines Carlo Collodi's classic tale of Pinocchio. Thrust into the grim and elegant Belle Époque era city of Krat, players awaken as a puppet created by Geppetto. Caught in a web of deceit, you must fight your way through streets slick with blood and madness.

Mechanics of the Soul

  • A Dark Retelling: Experience a twisted, grimdark narrative where morality is blurred and every truth is hidden behind a lie.
  • The Puppet Frenzy: Survive a once-prosperous, beautiful city that has collapsed into a living nightmare, overrun by deadly mechanized puppets and a devastating plague.
  • Dynamic Combat: Engage in punishing combat. Utilize a profound weapon-making system that allows you to dismantle and combine blades and handles to forge unique armaments suited to your playstyle.
  • The Lie System: You are a puppet, but you can choose to lie to comfort others or manipulate situations. These lies shape your humanity, altering the narrative path and leading to multiple devastating endings.
